Rooftop grid-tied

A rooftop grid-tied solar system is a practical and eco-friendly solution for generating electricity. It involves installing solar panels on the roof of a building and connecting them to the local power grid. These panels capture sunlight and convert it into electricity, which can be used to power appliances and electronics within the building. Any surplus electricity produced is automatically fed back into the grid, allowing the owner to earn credits or reduce their electricity bills through net metering programs. This setup not only helps to reduce the carbon footprint of the property but also offers potential cost savings over time.
